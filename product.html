Our Products

Hot Rolled Coil/Plate

Hot-rolled steel in the form of coils and plates is a type of steel product produced through

the hot rolling process. Manufacturers and users of this steel type commonly refer to this

product as 'black steel,' distinguishing it from cold-rolled steel sheets, also known as 'white

steel.

Cold Rolled Coil/Sheet

Cold-rolled steel, commonly known as 'white steel,' is a type of steel product produced

through the cold rolling process. This 'white steel' possesses distinct properties significantly

different from 'black steel' or hot-rolled steel sheets. Cold-rolled steel exhibits superior

surface quality, thinner gauges, precise dimensions, excellent mechanical properties, and

exceptional formability. Steel in this category is widely utilized in forming processes due to

its superior formability, weldability, and surface roughness quality.

Wire Rod

Wire rods are manufactured from steel billets, which categorizes them as long products,

distinguishing them from hot-rolled steel sheets and cold-rolled steel sheets made from

steel slabs. Wire rods are typically classified based on their carbon content: low, medium, or

high-carbon wire rods. Additionally, wire rods are categorized based on their specific

applications.
