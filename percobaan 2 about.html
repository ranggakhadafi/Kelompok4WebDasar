About Krakatau Steel

PT Tambang, established in the year 2000, stands as a prominent player in the mining industry. With a rich history and a commitment to excellence, we have emerged as a leading global supplier of high-quality minerals and metals.

Our dedication to quality, innovation, and sustainability sets us apart. We strive to meet the diverse needs of industries worldwide by delivering exceptional value through our products and services.

Our Core Values

Core Value Description
Quality We uphold the highest standards of quality in all our products.

Vision and Mission

Company Vision :

To be a leading producer of precious metals that values people, the environment, and positive contributions to society.

Company Mission :

1. Sustainable Mining: Conduct efficient and responsible mining operations, comply with international environmental standards, and optimize the use of natural resources.

2. Safety and Security: Ensure the safety and security of all employees, contractors, and the communities around the operational areas.

3. Community Empowerment: Empower local communities through social, economic, and educational development programs.

4. Product Quality: Produce high-quality products that meet the needs of the global market.

5. Innovation and Research: Continuously innovate in mining technology and conduct research to ensure business sustainability.
